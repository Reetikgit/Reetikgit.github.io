RULES

                                             
1.Participants are required to reach 
the stage / place of conduction of event
 10 mins prior to the commencement of 
 the event.

2.A track of 2 mins duration will 
be selected by the 
coordinator team well in advance and
 the participants
 will not get to choose or change 
 the track.

3.Each participant will get 2 rounds comprising of
 30 seconds each to showcase their dance moves and 
 after the completion of 30 seconds his/her opponent
  will perform for next 30 seconds to the same track
   as performed by the previous participant.

4.After the completion of each round the judge will 
choose the best of the two participants and they will
 be qualified to the next round .

5.In the final round 2 participants will compete 
against each other and the best of the two will 
be awarded as the winner .

6.The two people competing against each other
 will be selected randomly by the faculty in charge.

7.The judge's decision is final and breakage of any of 
the above rules, misconduct will lead to disqualification
 of the participants
