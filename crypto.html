About

Crypto-hunt is an event
inspired by the traditional Treasure Hunt but
involving concepts of technology. The clues
are hidden offline in the Sai Vidya campus as
well as online on the internet. Participants are
supposed to follow the clues and reach the
treasure.


RULES

1.Teams of 3 participants each.

2.Duration is 90 minutes.

3.The first clue will be given, students must
follow the clues and reach the treasure
which will be a part of the clues.

4.Use of mobile phones and computers is
allowed. It is preferred that the participants
have a smartphone with internet access and
camera functionality.

5.The coordinators can be contacted in case of
any issues.
6.The coordinators will not help regarding clues.

7.Tampering the physical clues hidden offline
will lead to immediate disqualification
from the event.
